Calculus
Basic Derivatives
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(c) = 0$ (constant rule)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(x^n) = nx^{n-1}$ (power rule)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(e^x) = e^x$ (exponential rule)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(\ln x) = \frac{1}{x}$ (natural logarithm)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(\sin x) = \cos x$ (sine)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(\cos x) = -\sin x$ (cosine)
  • $\frac{d}{dx}(\tan x) = \sec^2 x$ (tangent)
Basic Integrals
  • $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C$ (for $n \neq -1$)
  • $\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln|x| + C$
  • $\int e^x dx = e^x + C$
  • $\int \sin x dx = -\cos x + C$
  • $\int \cos x dx = \sin x + C$
  • $\int \sec^2 x dx = \tan x + C$
  • $\int \frac{1}{1+x^2} dx = \tan^{-1} x + C$
Derivative Rules
  • Sum Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}(f(x) + g(x)) = \frac{d}{dx}f(x) + \frac{d}{dx}g(x)$
  • Product Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}(f(x) \cdot g(x)) = f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x)$
  • Quotient Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}) = \frac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}$
  • Chain Rule: $\frac{d}{dx}(f(g(x))) = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)$
Applications of Calculus
  • Maximum: If $f'(c) = 0$ and $f''(c) < 0$, $f(c)$ is a maximum
  • Minimum: If $f'(c) = 0$ and $f''(c) > 0$, $f(c)$ is a minimum
  • Inflection Point: If $f''(c) = 0$ and $f'''(c) \neq 0$, $(c, f(c))$ is an inflection point
  • Area under curve from $a$ to $b$: $\int_a^b f(x)dx$
  • Arc Length: $L = \int_a^b \sqrt{1 + [f'(x)]^2} dx$
  • Volume of Revolution about x-axis: $V = \pi \int_a^b [f(x)]^2 dx$
Trigonometry
Fundamental Identities
  • $\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$
  • $\tan \theta = \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta}$
  • $\cot \theta = \frac{\cos \theta}{\sin \theta} = \frac{1}{\tan \theta}$
  • $\sec \theta = \frac{1}{\cos \theta}$
  • $\csc \theta = \frac{1}{\sin \theta}$
  • $1 + \tan^2 \theta = \sec^2 \theta$
  • $1 + \cot^2 \theta = \csc^2 \theta$
Angle Addition Formulas
  • $\sin(\alpha \pm \beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta \pm \cos \alpha \sin \beta$
  • $\cos(\alpha \pm \beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta \mp \sin \alpha \sin \beta$
  • $\tan(\alpha \pm \beta) = \frac{\tan \alpha \pm \tan \beta}{1 \mp \tan \alpha \tan \beta}$
Double Angle Formulas
  • $\sin 2\theta = 2\sin \theta \cos \theta$
  • $\cos 2\theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta = 2\cos^2 \theta - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 \theta$
  • $\tan 2\theta = \frac{2\tan \theta}{1 - \tan^2 \theta}$
Laws of Triangles
  • Law of Sines: $\frac{\sin A}{a} = \frac{\sin B}{b} = \frac{\sin C}{c}$
  • Law of Cosines: $c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos C$
  • Area of Triangle: $\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2}ab\sin C$
Half-Angle Formulas
  • $\sin \frac{\theta}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos \theta}{2}}$
  • $\cos \frac{\theta}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos \theta}{2}}$
  • $\tan \frac{\theta}{2} = \frac{\sin \theta}{1+\cos \theta} = \frac{1-\cos \theta}{\sin \theta}$
Product-to-Sum Formulas
  • $\sin \alpha \sin \beta = \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\alpha-\beta) - \cos(\alpha+\beta)]$
  • $\cos \alpha \cos \beta = \frac{1}{2}[\cos(\alpha-\beta) + \cos(\alpha+\beta)]$
  • $\sin \alpha \cos \beta = \frac{1}{2}[\sin(\alpha+\beta) + \sin(\alpha-\beta)]$
Mechanics
Linear Kinematics
  • Average Velocity: $\bar{v} = \frac{\Delta x}{\Delta t}$
  • Instantaneous Velocity: $v = \frac{dx}{dt}$
  • Average Acceleration: $\bar{a} = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t}$
  • Instantaneous Acceleration: $a = \frac{dv}{dt} = \frac{d^2x}{dt^2}$
  • Velocity with Constant Acceleration: $v = v_0 + at$
  • Position with Constant Acceleration: $x = x_0 + v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2$
  • Velocity-Position Relation: $v^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x - x_0)$
Forces and Motion
  • Newton's Second Law: $F = ma$
  • Weight: $W = mg$
  • Friction Force: $F_f = \mu N$ (where $\mu$ is coefficient of friction)
  • Work: $W = F \cdot d \cdot \cos\theta$
  • Kinetic Energy: $KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$
  • Potential Energy (gravitational): $PE = mgh$
  • Conservation of Energy: $KE_1 + PE_1 = KE_2 + PE_2$
Circular Motion
  • Angular Velocity: $\omega = \frac{\Delta \theta}{\Delta t}$
  • Linear Speed in Circular Motion: $v = r\omega$
  • Centripetal Acceleration: $a_c = \frac{v^2}{r} = r\omega^2$
  • Centripetal Force: $F_c = \frac{mv^2}{r} = mr\omega^2$
  • Angular Momentum: $L = I\omega$ (where $I$ is moment of inertia)
  • Torque: $\tau = r \times F = rF\sin\theta$
Fluid Mechanics
  • Density: $\rho = \frac{m}{V}$
  • Pressure: $P = \frac{F}{A}$
  • Hydrostatic Pressure: $P = P_0 + \rho gh$
  • Buoyant Force: $F_B = \rho_f gV$ (Archimedes' Principle)
  • Continuity Equation: $A_1v_1 = A_2v_2$
  • Bernoulli's Equation: $P_1 + \frac{1}{2}\rho v_1^2 + \rho gh_1 = P_2 + \frac{1}{2}\rho v_2^2 + \rho gh_2$
Dynamics
Impulse and Momentum
  • Linear Momentum: $p = mv$
  • Impulse: $J = F \Delta t$
  • Impulse-Momentum Theorem: $J = \Delta p$
  • Conservation of Momentum: $m_1v_{1i} + m_2v_{2i} = m_1v_{1f} + m_2v_{2f}$
  • Coefficient of Restitution: $e = \frac{v_{2f} - v_{1f}}{v_{1i} - v_{2i}}$
  • Perfectly Elastic Collision: $e = 1$
  • Perfectly Inelastic Collision: $e = 0$
Rotational Dynamics
  • Moment of Inertia of a Point Mass: $I = mr^2$
  • Moment of Inertia of a Thin Rod: $I = \frac{1}{12}mL^2$ (about center)
  • Moment of Inertia of a Disk: $I = \frac{1}{2}mR^2$ (about center)
  • Moment of Inertia of a Hollow Sphere: $I = \frac{2}{3}mR^2$ (about center)
  • Moment of Inertia of a Solid Sphere: $I = \frac{2}{5}mR^2$ (about center)
  • Rotational Kinetic Energy: $KE_{rot} = \frac{1}{2}I\omega^2$
  • Parallel Axis Theorem: $I = I_{cm} + md^2$
Oscillations and Simple Harmonic Motion
  • Position Equation: $x(t) = A\cos(\omega t + \phi)$
  • Angular Frequency: $\omega = 2\pi f = \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$
  • Period: $T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega} = 2\pi\sqrt{\frac{m}{k}}$
  • Frequency: $f = \frac{1}{T} = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = \frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}$
  • Spring Constant: $k = \frac{F}{x}$
  • Energy in SHM: $E = \frac{1}{2}kA^2$
Electrodynamics
  • Coulomb's Law: $F = k\frac{q_1q_2}{r^2}$
  • Electric Field: $E = \frac{F}{q} = k\frac{Q}{r^2}$
  • Electric Potential: $V = \frac{U}{q} = k\frac{Q}{r}$
  • Ohm's Law: $V = IR$
  • Electric Power: $P = VI = I^2R = \frac{V^2}{R}$
  • Lorentz Force: $F = qvB\sin\theta$
  • Faraday's Law: $\varepsilon = -\frac{d\Phi_B}{dt}$
